Why Flutter is Awesome?

What is Flutter and why I think it is the best way to develop cross-platform apps in 2019?

What is Flutter?

Flutter is free, open-source framework made by Google for building cross-platform apps.

It allows you to build beautiful native apps on iOS and Android, from single codebase.

Why Flutter

With Flutter, you can create beautiful, butter-smooth, performant apps faster than ever, without compromising on quality or performance.

Hot Reload

Sub-second reload times will allow you to quickly and easily experiment, build UIs, add features and fix bugs. Moreover, you'll not lose the state of the app after the reload.

You can test the apps on emulators, simulators or your Android or iOS smartphone.

You'll find this especially useful if you come from native Android development where you have to rebuild your app every time.

Great VS Code Support

Developers who doesn't have powerful machines will appreciate the fact that you can create and build your apps straight from the Visual Studio Code.

You don't even have to open the RAM eater Android Studio.

Of course it supports Inteliij IDEA, Android Stuido and XCODE too.

Great Tooling

Really clean and easy to learn Command Line Interface, and Dart support makes the development seamless.

Rich Set of Customizable Widgets

You will be amazed how much of components Flutter ships out of the box. From buttons, to beautiful animations. It makes app development faster than ever.

Expressive and Flexible UI

Flutter gives you complete control over every pixel on the screen by providing you easy to use widgets, rendering, animation and gestures APIs.

You have the flexibility to build very custom designs.
